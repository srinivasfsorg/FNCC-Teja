About FNCC

Film Nagar Cultural Centre (FNCC) is situated at Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The area is Film Nagar, after Jubilee Hills where most of the elite of Hyderabad have their homes.

A co-operative housing society was formed unique in the world for and by the Telugu Film Industry persons to have their homes for all categories like Producers, Technicians and Artists etc wherein, now there are facilities in the building of the society, along with society office, there are offices of A.P Film Chamber of Commerce, Movie Artists Association, Telugu Film Producer`s Council, Preview Theater, Offices of Some Producers, and also guest rooms for "Persons" from film industry from outside of Twin cities. Adjacent is a Temple Complex popular as "Film Nagar Diva Sannidhanam".

Film Nagar Cultural Centre (FNCC) was established with the main aim of encouraging the Telugu film fraternity in the vicinity and elsewhere too to have facilities similar to clubs, recreation to all connected to film industry and also to encourage activities connected to film, T.V and various cultural activities.

Welcome to the official website of the Film Nagar Cultural Centre (FNCC). Our journey began in 1993 with a vision to foster and empower the Telugu film industry and its community, both locally and beyond. Our primary goal was to provide a haven akin to clubs, offering recreation and facilities to everyone associated with the world of cinema. Moreover, we aimed to kindle and celebrate the diverse realm of film, television, and various cultural pursuits.


Commencing with a modest start, our center was established as a response to the growing needs of the Telugu film fraternity. With nearly 300 pioneering members at inception, FNCC has grown to encompass a thriving community of over 2500 members, including dependents. What began as an endeavor in a compact rented bungalow has now evolved into an expansive institution nestled atop a serene hillock, spanning across a sprawling 3 and 1/2 acres of land.


The evolution of Film Nagar Cultural Centre, from its humble origins to its current stature, is a testament to the unwavering support and enthusiasm of our members. Notably, our journey would not have been possible without the steadfast commitment of our donor members, whose generous contributions enabled us to embark on ambitious projects, including the establishment of our own dedicated building and a state-of-the-art sports complex.


At FNCC, we take immense pride in our history, accomplishments, and the vibrant community that has been instrumental in shaping our identity. As we continue to chart new horizons, we remain dedicated to our founding principles of nurturing the film fraternity, promoting creativity, and providing a platform for various cultural endeavors.


Thank you for visiting our website and being a part of our enriching journey. Together, we celebrate the art of cinema and the spirit of cultural unity at Film Nagar Cultural Centre
